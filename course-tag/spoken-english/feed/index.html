]]> The “Spoken English” course is designed to help you master the art of speaking English with confidence and fluency. Whether you’re a beginner looking to improve your conversational skills or an intermediate learner aiming to refine your pronunciation and expand your vocabulary, this course offers comprehensive training to enhance your spoken English abilities. Led by experienced language instructor Aleena, this course provides practical lessons, interactive exercises, and real-world practice to help you communicate effectively in English.

Throughout this course, you’ll engage in a variety of speaking activities, including dialogues, role-plays, and discussions, that will build your confidence and proficiency. By the end of the course, you’ll be able to speak English more naturally, express yourself clearly, and engage in conversations with ease in both personal and professional settings.

What You’ll Learn:

  1. Basic Conversational English:
    • Introducing yourself and others.
    • Common greetings and everyday expressions.
    • Asking and answering simple questions.
  2. Pronunciation and Accent Training:
    • Improving pronunciation of common English sounds.
    • Understanding and practicing intonation and stress.
    • Reducing accent and speaking more clearly.
  3. Building Vocabulary:
    • Expanding your vocabulary for everyday situations.
    • Learning essential words and phrases for various contexts.
    • Using idioms and expressions naturally in conversation.
  4. Grammar for Speaking:
    • Applying grammar rules in spoken English.
    • Constructing correct and effective sentences.
    • Understanding tenses, prepositions, and conjunctions.
  5. Conversational Practice:
    • Engaging in role-plays and dialogues.
    • Discussing common topics such as work, travel, and hobbies.
    • Practicing small talk and social conversations.
  6. Listening and Comprehension:
    • Developing listening skills for better understanding.
    • Responding appropriately to questions and statements.
    • Understanding different accents and dialects.
  7. Advanced Speaking Techniques:
    • Participating in debates and group discussions.
    • Expressing opinions, agreeing, and disagreeing politely.
    • Improving fluency and spontaneity in speech.
